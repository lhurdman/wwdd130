Content

Home page

Welcome to "Let's Work Out"! The purpose of this page is provide a list of exercises you can do in the gym.

It can be hard walking into a gym, wheather you do it every day or have never gone. It can be hard to know what to do. Even if you have been going to the gym your whole life, it is fun to mix it up sometimes. We provide a list of simple exersises that are easy to understand and perform.

We hope you find what you are looking for :) Exercises are separated into upper body and lower body.
